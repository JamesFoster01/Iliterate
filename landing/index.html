iliterate

Highlight text → right-click → get clean notes. Fast enough for essays. Sharp enough for exams.

How it works

  1. Highlight text on any webpage or online reading.
  2. Right-click → Go iliterate.
  3. Choose Essay or Exam mode, add a keyword if you want.
